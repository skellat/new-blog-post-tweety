1 Character Generation: Solar Winds

Solar Winds by Stellagama Publishing is available at https://www.drivethrurpg.com/en/product/508413/solar-winds. The item is available under CC BY 4.0 license and was written by Omer Golan-Joel. Excerpts are utilized below to highlight character creation steps I have taken. I very strongly urge you to buy a copy or five of this in print.

1.1 Character Creation

Use the following steps to create a Solar Winds character.

  1. 1.

    Think of a character concept. Do you want to play an intrepid interstellar explorer, an infamous rogue, a mad scientist, or someone else?

  2. 2.

    Allocate 5 points among the 7 skills, maximum 3 points in any given skill.

  3. 3.

    Choose an archetype (see below).

  4. 4.

    The Referee will determine what equipment your character can access. Gear depends on the game universe and the starting adventure.

  5. 5.

    Name your character.

1.3 Skills

Solar Winds has seven skills:

  • •

    Combat: fighting on ground and in space. Includes attacking foes but also other martial skills, such as taking cover under fire.

  • •

    Knowledge: general education and cognitive capabilities.

  • •

    Physical: brute strength and stamina, also including resistance to toxins and diseases and pain tolerance.

  • •

    Social: persuasion, deception, collecting information from humans and aliens, interrogation, and so on.

  • •

    Space: operating spacecraft and spacesuits; coping with micro-gravity conditions; and other matters pertaining to space exploration.

  • •

    Stealth: sneaking, hiding, picking pockets, and other acts of subterfuge.

  • •

    Tech: technological knowledge, from picking locks, to driving, to hacking sophisticated computer systems to repairing technological systems.

Skill ratings range from 0 (an inexperienced character) to 3 (a first-rate professional). In rare cases, certain characters will have a skill of 4 or 5, denoting world-class or even galactic-level professionalism!
